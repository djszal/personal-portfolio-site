Career

In 2013, I graduated Virginia Tech with a Bachelors of Science in Construction Engineering and Management. Upon graduation, I started down a path of developing my skills and learning from senior-level leaders to become a construction project manager. I slowly climbed the ranks and became a project manager in early 2017. I sucessfully managed a couple of dozen projects over those years by keeping them on schedule, on budget and within all stakeholder expectations. The skills I developed and learned were invaluable and ready to be transferred to the next chapter in my career.

In early 2023, I completed a fullstack coding bootcamp to step myself out into a new career path. I developed technical skills and knowledge in html, css, javascript, x, y, z. I had come into the bootcamp with a strong interest in coding, and I left the bootcamp more than excited to see how my future unfolds as a fullstack developer.
