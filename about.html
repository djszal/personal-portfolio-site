Career

Before entering the world of full stack development, I worked for 8+ years as a project manager in the construction industry, where I gained experience managing various client projects from start to finish. While I enjoyed helping a client's project go from paper conceptualization to a tangible product while working with a team of specialized professionals, I loved being able to use various technologies to make each one successful.


With these technologies, I would become immensely focused while using scheduling software to ensure the projects were on track or budgeting software to make sure budgets were being met or becoming proficient with other applications to help streamline communications between all stakeholders. As my proficiency with these platforms grew, I enjoyed sharing my knowledge of them with other colleagues to help their careers and projects succeed.


I soon found myself searching for ways to work more with technology in creative, challenging, team-oriented ways that benefited other people in various aspects of their lives. That search led me to complete a 3-month, full stack, web development program offered through Virginia Tech in order to jumpstart a career in the Tech industry.


I look forward to utilizing the skills I've acquired through the program, along with my previous experience, and bringing them to an organization where I can further grow professionally as a full stack developer and help an organization meet its goals.
